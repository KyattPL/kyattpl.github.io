# The problem
Learning Spanish verb conjugations can be tough. You can stare at cheat sheets all day, but active practice is key to making it stick. Manually writing out tables is tedious, and many online tools are cluttered, slow, or require subscriptions. You need a clean, fast, and interactive tool to drill conjugations until they become second nature.

# How to Use
Simply visit the website: [Link](https://kyattpl.github.io/conjukyatto/):
1.  The app starts with a random verb and tense to get you practicing immediately.
2.  Use the "Select verb..." dropdown to search for and choose a specific verb.
3.  Use the "Select tense/mood..." dropdown to focus on a particular conjugation pattern.
4.  Fill in the blanks in the interactive table.
5.  Click "Check" to get instant feedback on your answers, "Show Answers" to reveal the correct forms, or "Clear" to start over.
6.  Toggle "Multi-Tense Mode" to select and practice multiple tenses for the same verb side-by-side.

## Functionality

- **Interactive Conjugation Tables**: Actively type in your answers instead of just passively reading.
- **Single & Multi-Tense Modes**: Practice one tense at a time for focused learning, or compare multiple tenses to understand their differences.
- **Instant Feedback**: Buttons to "Check", "Clear", and "Show Answers" give you immediate control over your learning.
- **Randomization**: "Random Verb" and "Random Tense/Mood" buttons keep your practice sessions fresh and challenging.
- **Comprehensive Library**: Includes a wide range of common Spanish verbs across all major tenses and moods.
- **Clean & Responsive**: A modern, clutter-free interface that works on any device.
- **Everything runs in your browser** — no installation or dependencies required.

# Use cases

- **Beginners**: Drill the fundamental present, preterite, and imperfect tenses.
- **Intermediate Learners**: Tackle the subjunctive, commands, and conditional tenses.
- **Advanced Students**: Review and maintain speed and accuracy with less common forms.
- **Teachers & Tutors**: Use as an in-class tool or for assigning interactive homework.

# The Cheat Sheets
In addition to the interactive practice tool, the site also includes two comprehensive reference pages for offline study or quick lookups.

# Technical details

Built with Next.js, React, and TypeScript for a modern, interactive experience. Styled with Tailwind CSS and Shadcn/UI for a clean and responsive design. Verb data is loaded from a local JSON file, ensuring fast performance and offline potential.
